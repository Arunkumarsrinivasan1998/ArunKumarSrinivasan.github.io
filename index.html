Arun is a bit of a workaholic, but in the best possible way. His quirky personality and positive attitude make even the most challenging tasks seem like a fun challenge.



A graduate in B.Tech Information Technology from Chennai working as Application Development Senior Analyst at Accenture India. Involved in development of Micro services using Spring Boot and Go language. Implemented BDD framework Karate and Gatling for performance testing and process improvement. Worked as developer in Sterling IBM Integrator to develop interfaces for file transfers, encryption and decryption of files using secured protocols and archival of files on Google cloud Storage. Expertise in working on Big query and management of Storage Buckets on Google Cloud Platform.Experienced in Terraform, Jenkins, Open Shift, Bit Bucket, gitlab, Jira , Confluence and Oracle and MongoDB.
